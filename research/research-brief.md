# School Research Brief

## Inclusion criteria (from ranking-methodology.md)
- School uses the English National Curriculum
- Located in one of the top 100 commercial cities in Asia
- Year 3 tuition >= USD 18,000 equivalent
- School publishes fee information publicly
- Must have been open since 2025 or earlier
